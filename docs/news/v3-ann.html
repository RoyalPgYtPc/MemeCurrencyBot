V3 Feature Preview & Roadmap

Today we present an early preview of MemeCoin V3. This release is focused on long-term engagement, transparency, and personal progression.

Planned V3 Features

V3 is intentionally scoped to deliver high-impact quality-of-life and engagement systems that build on the stability delivered by V2 and the protection provided by Project Obsidian.

Primary Objectives

Introduce persistent progression, improve auditability, and provide richer reward pathways while preserving fairness.

Feature Overview

  • Achievement System (Phase 1): A lightweight badge system awarding milestones for participation.
  • Transfer History: A user-facing ledger for improved transparency and dispute resolution.
  • Quest System: Basic task loops to encourage recurring engagement.
  • Enhanced Shop: Expanded catalog of items and seasonal releases.
  • User Profiles: Public summary pages showing stats and achievements.
  • Notification System: Real-time alerts for earnings, penalties, and shop events.

Delivery Roadmap

  1. Internal design & specification sign-off.
  2. Development sprints: Achievement System & Transfer History.
  3. Quest System and Shop expansion deployment.
  4. Profiles and Notification System integration.
  5. Post-release monitoring and iterative community-driven improvements.

"V3 aims to make participation meaningful and visible — progression you can trust, and history you can verify."

— Lead Architect, MemeCoin Dev Team

Testing Program

Volunteers for development and testing are encouraged to reach out via mod mail. Priority will be given to testers who helped during V2.
