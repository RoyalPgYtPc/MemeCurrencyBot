MemeCoin V1 Launch

MemeCoin V1 is live. The initial release introduces a simple, fair economy with 10 flair tiers, daily bonuses, and a basic reward loop for participation.

What V1 Includes

  • 10 Flair Tiers: Clear progression milestones.
  • Daily Bonus: Consistent rewards for engagement.
  • Basic Rewards: Earn MC for community activity.
Evolution

V1 establishes a fair foundation that later versions build on with security, progression, and advanced systems.

What Comes Next

The next update will focus on stability improvements and early safeguards, paving the way for broader features in V2 and V3.
