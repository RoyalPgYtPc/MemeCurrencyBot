Dashboard V2.0 Official Release

MemeCoin Dashboard V2.0 has officially arrived. This update delivers our biggest expansion to date, introducing core economic systems, structured progression, and administrative tools.

The new V2 interface featuring advanced community analytics.

Overview

With V2.0, MemeCoin transitions from an experimental community reward mechanic to a stable economy with trackable progress, daily engagement incentives, and fairness protections.

Key Takeaway

V2.0 establishes a structured digital points economy for Reddit memers — with progression, bonuses, analytics, and governance tools.

Feature Highlights

  • Daily Bonus System: Claim 50 MC every 24 hours with server-side tracking.
  • User-to-User Transfers: Send MemeCoin securely with preset options.
  • Advanced Analytics: Earnings breakdown and flair progress indicators.
  • Flair Shop Revamp: Tier progression and instant flair application.
  • Leaderboard Update: Medals for top placements and personal highlights.

How It Works

  1. Earn MemeCoin from community posts and comments.
  2. Claim your daily login reward to build your balance.
  3. Redeem coins for tier-based flairs in the shop.
  4. Compete on the global community leaderboard.

The V2 upgrade transforms MemeCoin from a fun experiment into a structured economy powered by community creativity.

— MemeCoin Development Team

Technical Details

V2.0 includes a full TypeScript migration and optimised Redis usage. Systems such as cooldowns and earnings validation now run server-side to ensure economy integrity.
