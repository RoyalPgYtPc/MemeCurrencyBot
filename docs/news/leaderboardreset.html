Leaderboard Wipe:
Preparing for V2

The V1 leaderboard is being cleared to give every member a fair start in the new economy. Balances, flairs, and earned rewards remain untouched.

Oct 30 Wipe Date
Safe Balances
V2 Fresh Start
Fair For All

The era of MemeCoin Dashboard V1 is drawing to a close. To facilitate the transition to our highly anticipated V2.0 Ecosystem, we are conducting a full reset of the global leaderboard. This isn't just a technical necessity — it's a commitment to our growing community to provide a fair and competitive landscape from day one.

Why This Reset Matters

Throughout the V1 testing phase, the leaderboard captured data from various stages of development, including prototype earning models, early-bird participation, and developer stress tests.

By clearing these records, we ensure that the rewards and prestige of the V2 economy are earned under the new, balanced mechanics — including our refined daily bonus systems, optimized transfer protocols, and the upcoming Multiplier Events.

💡 What stays, what goes?

Don't panic — your wallet is safe. User balances, exclusive flairs, and total MemeCoin earnings remain completely untouched. Only the rankings and V1-specific performance metrics will be cleared to make room for V2 statistics.

Impacted Metrics

  • Global and regional leaderboard placements
  • Legacy rank history from the V1 era
  • Early testing performance badges (unless marked as 'Legacy')

A fresh start is the ultimate fairness. V2 isn't just an update — it's a new standard for the MemeCoin economy.

The Roadmap to V2

Once the clock hits zero, the V2 dashboard will go live globally. Here is what happens next:

  1. Calibration: The system will calculate initial eligibility based on V1 loyalty.
  2. Normalisation: All active users will start at the baseline rank tier.
  3. Ascension: New earning activities will immediately begin populating the fresh V2 leaderboard.

Technical Execution

The wipe will happen automatically during the maintenance window scheduled two hours before the V2 launch. No manual action is required from users. Simply log in once the V2 banner appears to claim your starting position in the new economy.
