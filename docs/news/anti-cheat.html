Project Obsidian: Integrity Standards

Built to preserve fairness and economic stability, Project Obsidian forms the core security foundation of the MemeCoin system.

Today we are officially unveiling Project Obsidian, the new anti-cheat and ecosystem protection platform developed exclusively for MemeCoin. Built to preserve fairness, trust, and long-term economic stability, Project Obsidian forms the core security foundation of the MemeCoin system going forward.

Mission & Purpose

Project Obsidian is designed to safeguard the MemeCoin economy by identifying, preventing, and responding to exploitative behavior. This includes currency farming, unfair trade activity, and attempts to manipulate the reward system.

💡 Core Principle

Integrity comes first — every user earns and progresses on equal terms. In an era of automated exploitation, we are committed to keeping the human element at the center of our community rewards.

Our Methodology

The system operates on a four-step lifecycle designed to be as non-intrusive as possible for legitimate users:

  1. Analysis: Passive monitoring of account and transaction patterns.
  2. Detection: Identification of behaviors that fall outside of 3-sigma standard deviations.
  3. Mitigation: Application of surgical corrections (warnings, deductions, or limitations).
  4. Review: Logging for manual oversight to ensure system accuracy.
“Project Obsidian is the backbone of a fair economy. Every user earns their place — nothing artificial, nothing hidden.”

Looking Ahead

Project Obsidian represents a long-term commitment to system equity, transparency, and trust. As MemeCoin continues to expand into V2 and beyond, Obsidian will remain the silent guardian ensuring the community grows on a foundation of genuine participation.

Thank you for supporting a secure and honest MemeCoin ecosystem.
