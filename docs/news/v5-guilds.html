Guilds & Treasuries: Team Progression

V5.0 introduces a fully cooperative layer to MemeCoin. Players can now create teams, pool resources in secure Treasuries, and progress through shared objectives.

What Guilds Enable

Guilds are designed to turn the economy into a shared experience. Team goals and collective rewards create a reason to coordinate and contribute beyond individual play.

  • Guild Treasuries: Shared MC pools that support team purchases and community events.
  • Team Quests: Cooperative objectives that require multiple members to complete for massive group rewards.
  • Seasonal Ranks: A separate leaderboard system that ranks Guilds based on their collective monthly performance.
  • Member Roles: Dynamic permissions allowing leaders to delegate spending and recruitment tasks.
⚖️ Fairness First

Treasuries are fully transparent. All deposits and withdrawals are recorded in a public guild ledger, governed by clear permissions to prevent any single member from draining team funds.

Why This Matters

V5 is built on long-term engagement. Guilds reward consistent effort, keep players collaborating, and give the community tools to create its own internal micro-economies and goals.
