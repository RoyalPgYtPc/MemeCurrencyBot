Feature Update November 6, 2025 3 min read

P2P Transfer System Live

Peer-to-peer transfers are now live. You can send MemeCoin directly to other users with optional notes, preset amounts, and full history tracking.

How Transfers Work

The new system handles balance checks, confirmation prompts, and audit logging to prevent mistakes. Every transfer is recorded in your activity log for transparent record keeping.

  • Simple Commands: Quick send flow with presets.
  • Transfer History: Each transaction is logged.
  • Safeguards: Prevents negative balances.
  • Notes: Optional messages for context.
Safety First

Transfers are screened by Project Obsidian to block suspicious activity and protect the economy.

Why This Matters

P2P transfers unlock community-driven rewards, friendly giveaways, and group initiatives. It also lays the groundwork for future V5 systems like Guild treasuries and collaborative events.
