Dashboard V4.0: The Final Evolution

The MemeCoin Development Team is pleased to confirm that Dashboard Version 4.0 has completed its final testing phase. This final patch introduces dynamic systems and advanced privacy controls.

12 Flair Tiers
900 Weekly MC Cap
Random Quest Engine
Solo Final Build

Economy & Progression Updates

Expansion of Flair Tiers

To support the growing economy, the flair system has been expanded from 10 to 12 tiers, introducing new endgame milestones.

  • Tier 11 – Cosmic Memer (50,000 MC): Includes a unique Cosmic Upvote icon.
  • Tier 12 – Divine Memer (100,000 MC): The new ultimate prestige rank.

Dynamic Quest Engine

The static quest system has been replaced with a Randomized Rotation Engine, ensuring more varied and engaging gameplay.

Quest Pool Details

8 Daily and 8 Weekly quests are now available, with 3 of each selected per reset. Weekly rewards now scale up to 900 MC.

Marketplace Enhancements

🏷️ Discount Visibility

Active discounts now display a “DISCOUNT ACTIVE” banner, a live countdown timer, and green-highlighted pricing for Flairs, Boosters, and Badges.

User Experience & Privacy

Advanced Profile Management

Users can now tap any leaderboard entry to view a public profile and control visibility for Account Balance, Activity Statistics, Asset Collections, and Daily Streaks.

Technical Patch Notes

• Optimized Redis caching & async data fetching
• Fixed analytics errors on empty leaderboards
• Improved profile state handling & navigation flow
• Quest color coding: Orange (Daily), Blue (Weekly)

Due to a teammate taking a well-deserved break, this version was finalized by a solo developer. Thank you for your patience during this cycle.
